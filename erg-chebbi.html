Rising to 150m and stretching 28km from North to South and 5-7km East to West, Erg Chebbi is a magical sight. Its accessibility and size mean it has been used in many films, such as The Mummy, Prince of Persia, and Spectre.

The dunes constantly shift due to the considerable desert winds, and local legend says that the dunes were sent as a punishment for turning away a weary traveler from the desert. They say that after they sent away this traveler, the gods made the dunes pile up right outside their town; to teach them a lesson and to never send away weary men and women ever again.
